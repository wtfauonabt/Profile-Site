We are proudly present you VSPHOENIX4 darts machine.

Divine Entertainment is the New Zealand distributor of PHOENIX Darts.

We provide network connected darts machines, which help different companies increase repeat visits, and attract new clientele through the promotion of PHOENIX Darts. Additionally, a portion of the revenue will be paid back to the establishment.

Setup is quick and easy. We simply need a power source and a internet connection, as players are connected to their online account when playing. Frequent tournaments are held to help promote PHOENIX Darts, which also increases sales on a regular basis.
